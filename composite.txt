1. The File is a Component, Text and Sound represent Leaf and the Folder is a composite. 
Everything in Unix systems is a file, and folders are unique files that house other files.

2. Drawable is a Component, Rectangle and Ellipse are Leaf and Group is Composite.
All rectangles and ellipses are drawable and we can also draw them in groups hence groups are made of rectangles and ellipses. 

3. We can have a car as a component, we can have an engine that is made up from other electrical and mechanical components as composite and 
these electrical and mechanical components as leafs.
